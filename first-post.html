The NBA Restart

The NBA is back in action after taking a long break due to the coronavirus. Since its restart a few days ago, each team has played three scrimmage games and one or two seeding games. Every team will play eight seeding games before the Playoffs start to decide which teams will compete for the Larry O'Brien trophy.
Twelve teams are already qualified for the playoffs, and these teams are: Milwaukee Bucks, Toronto Raptors, Boston Celtics, Miami Heat, Indiana Pacers, Philadelphia 76ers, Los Angeles Lakers, Los Angeles Clippers, Denver Nuggets, Utah Jazz, Oklahoma City Thunder and Houston Rockets (Click here to watch the NBA standings)
