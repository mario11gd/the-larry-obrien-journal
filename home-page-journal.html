About us

The Larry O'Brien is a weekly journal about the NBA. We update it every Sunday with all the matches results and the most important news of the week.
Larry O'Brien was the NBA commissioner for nine years (1975-1984). While he was the commissioner he helped expand the NBA from 18 to 23 teams, he ushered in the adoption of the three point line and he also negotiated to set up better cable TV contracts.
Because of this, the league decided to change the championship trophy's name to the Larry O'Brien Championship Trophy.
